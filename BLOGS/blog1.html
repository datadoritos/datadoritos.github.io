Our Team

Farhan Arif – Project Lead & Data Strategy

Farhan takes charge of defining the overall project goals, managing the timeline, and ensuring that the team’s efforts align with the project’s objectives. He focuses on the strategic aspect of data analysis—selecting appropriate analysis methods, identifying key performance indicators (KPIs), and overseeing the integration of insights into actionable business decisions.

Maaz Ahmed – Data Exploration & Feature Engineering

Maaz specializes in data exploration, identifying initial patterns, and performing feature engineering to enhance model performance. His tasks involve cleaning and transforming raw data into a usable format, as well as performing exploratory data analysis (EDA) to understand trends and relationships in the data before deep analysis.

Sana Zulfiqar – Data Visualization & Reporting

Sana handles the creation of dashboards, charts, and reports that communicate insights effectively to stakeholders. Her expertise in tools like Tableau, Power BI, or Python’s visualization libraries ensures that the data is presented in a way that is both visually appealing and informative.

Atfa Fatima – Advanced Analysis & Modeling

Atfa applies machine learning algorithms, statistical techniques, and hypothesis testing to uncover deeper insights from the data. She works on building predictive models, evaluating their performance, and ensuring they provide valuable, data-driven solutions to the problem at hand.

Ayesha Anwer – Data Quality & Cleaning

Ayesha ensures that the data is clean, accurate, and reliable. She handles data cleaning, validation, and optimization to prepare datasets for analysis. Ayesha ensures that there are no inconsistencies or missing values, helping the team avoid errors that could affect the quality of the insights.

Mazhar Iqbal – Data Storytelling & Client Presentations

Mazhar is responsible for presenting the final projects to clients, using data storytelling techniques to communicate complex findings in a compelling and clear way. His role focuses on crafting narratives around the data that make it easier for clients to understand and act on. Mazhar combines visualizations, context, and strategic insights to deliver engaging presentations that highlight the value and impact of the team’s work.
